## The starting point

Molly Maid of Oak Park had a service people love and a referral base to prove it —
but a quiet social presence and a lead-handling process that depended on whoever
could get to the phone first. Inquiries that came in after hours waited until morning.
By then, some had already booked with someone else.

## What we did

- **Done-for-you content.** A monthly content system — scripted, filmed, edited, and
  posted — that shows the work, the team, and the results. Consistency the algorithm
  (and the audience) rewards.
- **Instant lead response.** When someone fills out a form or sends a DM, an
  n8n-powered workflow answers immediately with a personalized response — even at
  2 AM. No lead sits waiting.
- **Follow-up sequences.** Multi-step text and email flows nurture leads who didn't
  book on first touch. Professional, consistent, and completely hands-off.
- **Reviews on autopilot.** After every job, happy customers automatically get a
  review request. More reviews, better local SEO, more trust.
